Grandma’s Secret Apple Pie

One bite, and everything changes...

Ingredients

  • 6–7 Granny Smith apples
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1 pie crust (top and bottom)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix apples with sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, flour, and lemon juice.
  3. Line pie dish with crust and fill with apple mix. Dot with butter.
  4. Cover with top crust. Crimp edges, slice vents, brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake for 50–60 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  6. Cool before serving. But wait... there’s one last thing...
